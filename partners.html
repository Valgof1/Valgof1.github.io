BDO

Accounting, tax, consulting and business advisory

BDO is a consultancy company active internationally. It has a strong reputation in Financial Consultancy (Audit, Corporate Finance, Accounting, Tax & Legal) and in other fields of personalized consultancy such as Strategy and Organisation, People, Interim Management, Digital and Risk. Their clients portfolio is very diversified: from big multinationals to family companies and private clients. The approach of BDO is pragmatic, upright and result-oriented. The company cares about the needs and demands of the clients. Each client has one and only contact person and a BDO office nearby. In Belgium, BDO has 800 partners and collaborators based all over the county. BDO has 11 offices situated in Antwerp, Brussels Airport, Brussels (2), Gent, Hasselt, La Hulpe, Liège, Malines, Namur-Charleroi and Roulers. Worldwide, BDO has more than 75.000 partners and collaborators and is active in 162 countries.

Ardent Group

Entertainment, communication and commercial real estate

Since its foundation, the Liège Ardent Group (formerly known as the Circus Group) has been driven by a passion to create and share with others its innovative projects and concepts that trigger curiosity, create extraordinary experiences and brighten up people’s everyday life. Ardent’s proximity, mentally as well as physically, to the general public has established its aim to provide a service that both incorporates and generates its key values: fun and entertainment. The group’s openness and confidence in the future allows it to innovate, to create and to develop in complete harmony and balance. Today the Ardent Group is present in the fields of entertainment, communication and commercial real estate, either directly or via partner companies. In the future it will develop in other countries and through new activities continually driven by the same mindset. The group has now more than 800 employees. It owns 30 gaming rooms, the casinos of Spa and Namur, three Casinos in France and launched several online gaming platforms (Circus.be, 777.be, Pokerstar.be)
