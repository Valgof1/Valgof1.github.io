Participation

1. The HEC Liège Business Game is only open to students officially enrolled in a school or a university in their third year of Bachelor, first year of Master or last year of Master (international students included).

2. The number of participants is limited to 100 students (50 teams of 2 students).

3.The participation in the HLBG is subject to online electronic acceptance of the Terms and Conditions and of the documentation concerning the assignment of the intellectual property and image rights.

4. The participation price is 20 euros and 10 euros will be reimbursed if you are well present at the event. This price includes all the food and beverages you will need during the two days.

5. The jury’s members will be partners of HEC.

5. By registering for the HLBG you accept that your data will be shared with our partners.
