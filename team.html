14 students from HEC Liège are working close together to provide you the best Business Game experience.

Our Team

The HEC Liège Business Game Team is composed of students from HEC Liège and the University of Liège. These students have different backgrounds such as bachelors or masters in law, economics, finance, banking or engineering.

The team's goal is to provide competitors with fun and challenging cases but also opportunities to expand their network thanks to partnerships with global companies.
